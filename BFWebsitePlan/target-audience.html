Target Audience:

The target audience that I would like to reach would idealy be located or traveling through the city of Carlsbad. Since we are a healthy drink shop, it would be nice to target people who are active to looking for something to make them feel healthier. I think that if we focus on those groups of people, we will attract customers who get what they want.

Personas:

Persona: Active People

The first persona that I think Bountiful Foods identifies with the most is an active person. Perhaps this person works out a lot or just watches what they put in their body. Either way, this persona exemplifies what the perfect consumer is for Bountiful Foods.

Persona: Familes on Vacation

Familes on a vacation and looking for a yummy treat are the perfect customer for Bountiful Foods. With a beach front location, it is easy to the family to come up to the shop for a quick drink before returning to splash around in the water.
