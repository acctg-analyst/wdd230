Bountiful Foods

Site Purpose:

Bountiful Foods is a fresh foods drink creator. They have been around for a very long time (1920) and have a variety of drinks. Bountiful Foods aims to help people find drinks that help energize them and propel them throughout their day. We inspire creativity in your life by allowing you to control what you drink with our custom drink option. We hope to eventually grow and spread through the valley. However, the first step in doing this is building a website to attact a larger market. We hope to expand our marketing efforts and take in a larger market share in the healthy drink industry.
