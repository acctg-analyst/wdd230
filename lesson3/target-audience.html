Target Audience:

The taget audience that I am hoping to reach here is an audience that is looking to gain more information about the city of Boise. Generally speaking, that will be adults both male and female. From there we could narrow it down to business people and those looking to explore the city more. Perhaps this could encompass travelers as well as local business persons and family members.

Personas:

Occupation: Local Small Business Owner

The first Persona that I figured would represent our target audience, would be a small business owner. This group would be interested in the networking portion of the website. It would also allow the business to connect with the city.

Occupation: A Traveler

The second persona that I figured would use this website would be a traveler. Often times, those traveling through the city are interested in knowing the events that are going on. They could also use this to find what makes the city unique.

Scenarios:

  1. What are the local business in my city?
  2. Do we have any events coming up?
  3. What would be a fun place to take my family?
  4. What makes our city unique?
  5. Does anyone in the city provide this specific service?
